Life in Lamoni

Linden Street Coffee House

Linden Street Coffee House is a family owned business here in downtown Lamoni. They are known to have the best coffee in town and the atmosphere is a great place to kick back and relax, chat with friends, or complete any work that needs to be done, all while enjoying your favorite warm (or cold!) beverage.

The Coliseum

This town movie theatre was built in 1911 and was first used by the University to hold student recitals and performances. It now holds one theatre where newer movies are played and concessions are offered! It is a very fun and affordable way to pass time with friends and see the newest movie that you've been wanting to see.

Graceland University

This beautiful private college was founded in 1895 and currently holds about 1,000 full time students. There are over 50 academic programs offered and the campus offers 19 different varsity sports. It has been recognized as a top school in the Midwest by the Princeton Review. It is a very friendly campus, so don't be suprised when a student smiles and waves at you!

Livestock Auction

This auction barn has been in business for over 60 years and is family owned. Sale day happens every Thursday and along with selling cattle, there is a cafe inside the Auction Barn that offers an amazing and affordable breakfast and lunch.

Lamoni Community Center

The community center is a great space for people who need a friendly location to meet up. There are two seperate meeting rooms and a kitchen that connects to a dining room. There is tons of space and it is a perfect venue for family gatherings, business meetings, birthday parties, and many more!
